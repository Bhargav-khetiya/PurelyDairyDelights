    Go to your Google Account settings page: https://myaccount.google.com/security

    Under "Signing in to Google", click on "App passwords".

    You may need to verify your identity by entering your Google account password.

    Scroll down to the "App passwords" section and select "Other (Custom name)".

    In the "Custom name" field, enter a name for your application (e.g., "Django SMTP").

    Click "Generate".

    Google will generate a unique 16-character application-specific password for you. Copy this password.

    Use this generated password instead of your regular Google account password in your Django application's email settings.
